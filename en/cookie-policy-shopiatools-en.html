What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on your device when you visit them. They are used to remember your preferences, maintain active sessions, and collect anonymous statistical information.

ShopIA Tools is an AI-powered ecommerce tools platform operating from Spain, compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the Spanish Organic Law on Data Protection (LOPD-GDD), and the Spanish Information Society Services Law (LSSI-CE).

Why don't we need consent for Plausible?

Plausible Analytics is a privacy-friendly web analytics tool that does not install cookies on your device and does not collect any personally identifiable data. All data is completely anonymous and processed on servers within the European Union.

Since no personal data is processed, consent is not required under the GDPR or the ePrivacy Directive.

Managing your consent

When you first visit shopiatools.com, a cookie management banner will appear where you can accept, reject or customize which services are activated. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie preferences" link in the page footer.

Rejecting non-technical cookies does not affect your ability to browse the website.
